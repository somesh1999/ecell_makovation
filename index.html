MAKOVATION v2.0
6th-7th October 2018

Makovation is a moderated hackathon which will run over a course of two days where students can bring out their innovative ideas to the floor which can hear and support them in the future. It is meant to spread awareness about entrepreneurship in the campus.

Register

Segments

Ideation Phase

This is targeted for 1st year students specifically because they are freshers and it is highly unlikely that they’d have a working model/Prototype ready this soon. (Cash prize will be lower than the working model segment)

Working Phase

This is a targeted segment for students in 2nd year and above. Here the students, either in groups or individually would be presenting a working model at the end of the event on which they will be marked and judged upon.

Note:

The segments are not binding to the year wise students and anyone can participate in any of the two segments. It is just a suggestion from the organizing body which has been made in view of tapping the freshers who can become the driving entrepreneurial force and motivation for the college in the coming years

what are the secret ingredients?

1. What is a "hackathon" and how much it will cost ?
business needs.

A hackathon is an event where teams of 2-5 people collaborate on a problem statement or an idea of their own within the specified time limit. Prizes are awarded to the most creative, polished and theme-specific projects or "hacks". The winners will be chosen by a panel of judges comprised of top industry professionals.
And it is COMPLETELY FREE

2. What if I don't have any experience?

No worries! Hackathons are really a place for learning. We will have series of workshops to bootstrap you for hacking so you'll be ready in no time. Mentors will be walking around around to help you with any problems you'll face. By the time you walk out, you'll have a new skill under your belt !

3. What happens if I don't have a team and is it limited to only coders ?

Dont worry! A lot of hackers just like you are in the same position. On the first day of the event, we will make sure to pair everyone up with a team of hackers so no one gets left out! There will also be an opportunity to connect with people and form teams before the event (after you have choosen to attend). And it is not at all limited to coders Makovation is all about promoting the idea of problem solving. Solutions are what we are looking for to the problems plaguing the society

4. How big
can a team be ?

Teams are usually 2-5 people in size. Teams bigger than 5 people are usually split up into smaller ones as we have found large teams aren't the best solution for a 24th event. Don't worry if you can't get find your team beforehand, volunteers at event will help you find your teammates and provide you with many opportunities to find others to help !
